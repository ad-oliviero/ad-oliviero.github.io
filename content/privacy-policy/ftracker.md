## Privacy policy

At Finance Tracker, we do not collect any personal information from our users.

The app uses Google APIs, so you are required to log in using your Google account.

We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe, but Google might collect data about your use of the app, which will be reported to us anonymously.
