A cross-platform Flutter application for converting between many kinds of measurement
units. A single Dart codebase runs across Android, iOS, web, Linux, macOS and Windows.

- One Flutter/Dart codebase deployed to six platforms
- Broad coverage of measurement-unit categories
- Live monetary exchange rates for currency conversion
