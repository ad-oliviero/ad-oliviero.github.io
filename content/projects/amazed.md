A macOS and Apple TV maze racing game that turns your iPhone into a controller.
<!-- more -->
AmazeD is a couch-multiplayer maze racing game built in Unity that runs on either macOS or 
Apple TV. The game lives on the big screen as a multi-player, up to four players, split-screen race.
Each player pairs an iPhone over the local network and uses it as a motion controller.
