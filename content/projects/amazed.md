A macOS and Apple TV maze racing game that turns your iPhone into a controller.
<!-- more -->
AmazeD is a party game built for game nights. Up to four players race through a dark, enchanted labyrinth. Each one steering their wizard by tilting their iPhone like a steering wheel. The maze fills your screen in split-screen, and every round generates a brand-new layout.

Your wizard's torch casts a dim glow through thick fog, and glittering stardust is the only hint that something lurks around the next corner. Navigate by instinct, or by listening to your friends when they hit a dead end.

The labyrinth is full of surprises.

Every maze is unique, every round is a fresh sprint into the unknown. The first wizard to find the glowing portal wins.

Just launch the game, pick up your iPhones, and play. No extra controllers needed. Just chaos, laughter, and the race to escape.
